    Mindfulness Exercises

    Practice meditation and breathing techniques.

    🌬️ Deep Breathing: Inhale slowly, exhale gently.

    🧘 Meditation: Focus on your breath, let thoughts pass.

    🎵 Sound Therapy: Listen to calming music or nature sounds.

    👁️ Visualization: Imagine a peaceful place to relax.

    📿 Mantra Repetition: Repeat a soothing word or phrase.

    ☀️ Stretching: Loosen up with simple morning moves.

    🤲 Gratitude: Write down three good things daily.

    🚶 Walking: Walk slowly, feel each step.

    🌳 Nature Time: Spend a few moments outdoors.

    📱 Digital Detox: Take a break from screens.
